What to Expect

More Than a Conference. A Coaching Institution.

The VBCA Annual Convention is the one day a year when Virginia baseball coaches — from youth programs to college staffs, from Northern Virginia to the Southwest — are all in the same building. The programming is built for coaches, by coaches: clinics led by practitioners, speakers who know what a dugout actually looks like, and peer conversations that don't happen anywhere else.

The evening reception doubles as the VBCA Hall of Fame induction ceremony — one of the most meaningful nights in Virginia baseball coaching. Tickets are open to the public.

  • Coaching clinics and educational sessions
  • Featured speakers from across the coaching profession
  • Hall of Fame induction ceremony and reception
  • Peer networking with coaches at every level
  • Regional committee gatherings
  • Convention photography and archives

The Ceremony

The Hall of Fame Induction — The Crown Jewel of the Convention.

The VBCA Hall of Fame induction ceremony takes place during the Annual Convention evening reception. This is Virginia baseball's highest coaching honor — and the most formally celebrated night of the VBCA calendar. Coach of the Year and the Baseball Impact Award are also presented at the ceremony.

The reception is open to the public. Tickets available at VBCA.org.
