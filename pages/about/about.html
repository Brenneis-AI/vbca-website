The Virginia Baseball Coaches Association is a 501(c)3 nonprofit professional organization built for baseball coaches at every level across the commonwealth.

Our Story

Built by Coaches, for Coaches.

Virginia baseball coaches had a problem: there was no statewide organization that represented them. No annual gathering that brought the commonwealth's coaching community together. No institution that preserved the legacy of coaches who shaped the game across generations of Virginia players.

Tim Merry changed that. In 2018, he founded the Virginia Baseball Coaches Association with a straightforward mission: to unite, educate, develop and support the coaches who make Virginia baseball what it is. Not just high school coaches or college coaches — every coach, at every level, across every corner of the state.

"Coaches make an incredible impact in so many ways."

Tim Merry, Founder & Executive Director

Eight years later, the VBCA has inducted 36 coaches into its Hall of Fame, established an annual convention at UVA Darden School of Business, and built a statewide network that covers Central, South, Valley, and West regions. The organization is affiliated with the American Baseball Coaches Association at the national level — giving VBCA members statewide community and national standing.

Four Pillars. One Direction.

Unite

Virginia baseball is strongest when its coaches know each other. VBCA builds the relationships and regional structures that keep the commonwealth's coaching community connected — across levels, across geographies, across programs.

Educate

The game improves when coaches improve. VBCA provides the clinics, conventions, and resources that keep Virginia coaches learning — from peer-to-peer knowledge sharing to nationally recognized speaker programming.

Develop

Development is not a destination. VBCA creates the pathways — professional development, event participation, career networking, and resource access — that let Virginia coaches grow throughout their careers, not just at the start of them.

Support

Every coach who joins VBCA enters a community that has their back. Support means resources, recognition, advocacy, and the quiet knowledge that when you show up to the Annual Convention, you are among peers.

National Affiliation

Statewide and National — Together.

The VBCA is an official affiliate of the American Baseball Coaches Association (ABCA) — the national professional organization for baseball coaches. The VBCA x ABCA joint membership gives Virginia coaches statewide community through the VBCA and national standing through the ABCA, at a combined rate of $75 per year.
