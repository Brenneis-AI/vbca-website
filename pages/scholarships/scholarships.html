VBCA Scholarship Program

Investing in the Future of Virginia Baseball.

The VBCA Scholarship Program helps coaches and players access the educational opportunities that make them better at the game — and better in life. Every dollar goes directly to those who need it.

Make a Tax-Deductible Donation

The Program

Built Into Every Membership. Strengthened by Every Donation.

Ten percent of every VBCA membership fee is automatically directed to the Scholarship Program. This means that every coach who joins VBCA — at any tier — contributes to a fund that opens doors for coaches and players who might otherwise go without the educational opportunities that define careers.

The VBCA Scholarship Program assists coaches and players in attending conventions, clinics, camps, and educational baseball events. The focus is access: breaking down the financial barrier between a coach or player and the development they deserve.

The Virginia Baseball Coaches Association is a 501(c)3 nonprofit organization. All donations are tax-deductible.

Coaches and Players — Both.

For Coaches

Coaching development doesn't stop when your career begins. Scholarships assist coaches in attending the VBCA Annual Convention, educational clinics, ABCA events, and professional development programs that make them better at what they do.

For Players

Access to elite development camps, college visits, and competitive showcases can be the difference in a player's trajectory. VBCA scholarships help ensure that financial limitation doesn't determine who gets the opportunity.

Are You a Coach or Player Seeking Scholarship Assistance?
