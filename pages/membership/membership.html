VBCA Membership

Your Coaching Career Deserves a Professional Home.

Join the Virginia Baseball Coaches Association and connect with coaches at every level across the commonwealth — starting at $25 per year. Membership runs September 1 through August 31.

Join Today See Member Benefits

Why It Matters

What Coaching Without a Community Costs You.

Coaching in isolation isn't just lonely — it limits you. Without a statewide network, you miss the clinics that sharpen your approach. Without peer learning, you repeat mistakes your colleagues already solved. Without a professional organization behind you, your career has no institutional home.

Virginia baseball coaches build programs that define communities. They deserve more than a solo career in a sport that demands teamwork at every level.

—

No statewide peer network means no shared knowledge — you're solving problems alone that others solved years ago.

—

Without professional development pathways, coaching credentials stay informal and individual — never collective or recognized.

—

Without an organization behind you, your voice in Virginia baseball policy is exactly zero.

"Every year without a VBCA membership is a year outside the room where Virginia baseball decisions get made."

Choose Your Membership.

  • VBCA member job board access

  • VBCA Zoom clinics and educational events
  • Statewide coach network
  • 10% of your fee funds the VBCA Scholarship Program

  • All Individual membership benefits for every staff member registered

  • Statewide network access for your entire coaching staff

What Membership Unlocks

Your Seat at the Table of Virginia Baseball.

As a VBCA member, you're connected to the full scope of what the Virginia Baseball Coaches Association does — from the annual convention floor to the regional committee table.

Annual Convention

December, UVA Darden School of Business — clinics, speakers, Hall of Fame ceremony, and peer networking

Virginia All-Star Game

Nominations, coach participation, and recognition at Virginia's premier senior showcase

Job Board

Access to open coaching positions across Virginia — youth, high school, and collegiate

Resource Library

Coaching PDFs, clinic recordings, convention content, and drill libraries (Phase 2)

Hall of Fame Eligibility

Retired coaches are eligible for nomination to the VBCA Hall of Fame — the commonwealth's highest coaching honor

Regional Committees

Active network in your region: Central, South, Valley, and Western Virginia

Zoom Clinics

Expert instruction, live Q&A, and continuing education — no travel required

ABCA Partnership

Discounted dual membership with the national American Baseball Coaches Association (Joint tier)

Statewide Network

Peer connections across every level — youth through college

Built Into Every Membership

Ten Percent of Every Dollar Goes to Coaching Scholarships.

When you join the VBCA, 10% of your membership fee is automatically directed to the VBCA Scholarship Program — a fund that helps coaches and players attend educational baseball events they couldn't otherwise afford. Membership isn't just an investment in yourself. It's an investment in the game.
