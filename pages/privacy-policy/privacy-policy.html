Policy Document

Privacy Policy

The Virginia Baseball Coaches Association ("VBCA," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of our members, visitors, and supporters. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you visit our website at vbca.org (the "Site").

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Name, email address, school or organization affiliation, and phone number when you sign up for our newsletter or SMS updates
  • Name, email address, and message content when you submit our contact form
  • Payment information collected through Givebutter when you complete a membership registration or donation (note: VBCA does not store payment information directly — all transactions are handled by Givebutter's secure payment platform)

We also collect certain information automatically when you visit the Site, including IP address, browser type, and pages visited, through standard web analytics tools.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process membership registrations and donations
  • Send VBCA news, event announcements, and coaching resources to subscribers
  • Respond to your questions and inquiries
  • Improve our website and member experience
  • Maintain records required for our 501(c)3 nonprofit status

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Third-Party Services

Our Site uses the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Givebutter — membership payments and donations givebutter.com
  • Google Maps — venue maps on Convention and All-Star Game pages google.com/maps
  • Social Media Platforms — embedded feeds and outbound links to X/Twitter, Instagram, and Facebook
  • Email / SMS platform — newsletter and communication delivery [Platform TBD — client to provide before go-live]

Cookies

Our Site may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve your browsing experience and analyze Site traffic. You may set your browser to refuse cookies, though some features of the Site may not function properly as a result.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, comply with our legal obligations, and resolve disputes. Members who wish to update or delete their information may contact us at tmerry@vbca.org.

Contact for Privacy Requests

For questions about this Privacy Policy or to request access, correction, or deletion of your personal information, contact:

Virginia Baseball Coaches Association
Email: tmerry@vbca.org
