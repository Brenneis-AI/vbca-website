Leadership

The People Behind Virginia Baseball Coaches Association.

The VBCA board is composed of active and former coaches who represent all levels and regions of Virginia baseball. They built this organization because they believed it needed to exist — and they run it because they believe it deserves to last.

Coaches come together to unite, educate, develop and support coaches across the commonwealth.

VBCA Board of Directors

Tim Merry

Founder & Executive Director

Midlothian, VA

Tim Merry founded the Virginia Baseball Coaches Association in 2018 with a simple but consequential conviction: that Virginia baseball coaches deserved a professional organization built around their needs. As Executive Director, he leads the VBCA's day-to-day operations, strategic direction, and member relationships.
